🤖 Bot Mode & Anti-Cheat

We have introduced a spectator mode for viewing our advanced A* bot in action. You can launch it via the command line with --bot.

To prevent players from toggling the bot to achieve illegitimate high scores, any game in which the bot has been activated will automatically save its high score under the [BOT] pseudonym.
